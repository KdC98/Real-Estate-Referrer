How it works

Introduce a buyer interested in Dubai property and earn 25% of our agent commission. No licence required.

1

Sign up (2 minutes)

Create your free account, fill in your details, and sign the referrer agreement online. No licence needed — the program is open to everyone.

2

Find a buyer

Talk to your network. Anyone interested in buying property in Dubai is a potential lead — wherever you are in the world.

3

Submit your lead

Log in to your dashboard, click "Add Lead", and fill in your client's details. That's all we need to get started.

4

Track in real time

Follow your lead's progress through each stage — New, Visit, Offer, Sold — with full transparency on your commission.

5

Get paid

Once the sale closes, your commission is calculated automatically and paid within 45–60 days. You earn 25% of our agent commission on every buyer transaction.

What makes a good lead?

Qualified lead

  • •Genuinely wants to buy in Dubai
  • •Has a clear budget in mind
  • •Looking to buy now, not in two years
  • •Easy to reach with accurate contact details
  • •Knows what they're looking for

Unqualified lead

  • •Just curious, not seriously interested
  • •No idea of their budget, or unrealistic
  • •Looking far into the future (1 year+)
  • •Unreachable or fake contact details
  • •Doesn't know what they want
