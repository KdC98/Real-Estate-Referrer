Recitals

WHEREAS, the Agency is engaged in the business of real estate brokerage – Selling and Buying of Properties in the UAE.

and,

WHEREAS, External Agent desires to refer potential clients ("Referrals") to the Agency in exchange for a referral fee on any revenue generated by the Agency as a result of such introductions.

NOW, THEREFORE, the parties hereto agree as follows:
  1. Throughout the duration of this agreement, the External Agent shall be responsible for introducing potential clients to the Agency who are interested in buying, selling, or renting properties in the UAE.
  2. The Agency shall bear no liability for any contracts, obligations, agreements, sales contracts, or legally binding documents entered into between the Referrer and any of its clients.
  3. In the event of a successful conclusion of a deal with the referred client, the Agency has agreed to share a portion of the commission it has earned with the External Agent. The distribution of this commission shall be based on the following terms:
    Commission structure by lead type:
    • 25% of the Agent's share of total commission for Buyer Leads (client looking to purchase)
    • 20% of the Agent's share of total commission for Seller Leads, Landlord Leads, and Tenant Leads
    • The commission will be calculated based on the total value of the property.
    • The referral fee owed to the External Agent shall be disbursed through bank transfer within four (4) weeks following the receipt and clearance of the commission by the Agency's bank account. The payment method and details, including account information for bank transfers, shall be agreed between both parties. In the event of any delay or discrepancy in the commission payment, the parties shall promptly communicate and resolve the issue to ensure the timely disbursement of the referral fee to the External Agent.
  4. This agreement will not be limited to Direct Selling from Developers; it may cover market reselling should the introduced client(s) be interested in market re-sale.
  5. The Agency reserves the right to make changes to this document or its financial scheme structure at any point of time, if it deems it necessary, by notifying the other party in writing when and if those changes take place.
  6. The terms and conditions of this agreement are confidential between the parties and shall not be disclosed to anyone else. The referrer agrees that this contract is confidential between the Agency and the External Agent.
  7. The duration of this contract is 1 year from the signing of this agreement and can be renewed upon the mutual agreement of both parties. The Agency reserves the right to terminate this agreement by providing one (1) week notice to the External Agent.
Disclaimer: The payment of commission by the developer is always subject to changes based on Developer's policies and procedures.
