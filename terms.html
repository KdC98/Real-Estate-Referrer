2. Definitions

  • Referrer / Business Introducer: An individual who registers on the Platform to refer potential clients (leads) to the real estate agent.
  • Lead: A potential client referred by a Referrer who is interested in buying, selling, renting, or leasing real estate in Dubai.
  • Qualified Lead: A lead with genuine interest, defined budget, complete contact information, and active timeline (0-6 months).
  • Agent: The licensed real estate professional operating under Solo Gate Real Estate (Alliance by EMAAR).
  • Commission: The payment due to the Referrer upon successful completion of a real estate transaction.
  • Transaction: A completed sale or rental agreement facilitated through a referred lead.

4. Commission Structure

4.1 Commission Rates

Referrers earn a commission based on the type of lead referred:

  • üèÜ Buyer (Sale): 25% of the agent's commission
  • Seller (Sale): 20% of the agent's commission
  • Landlord (Rental): 20% of the agent's commission
  • Tenant (Rental): 20% of the agent's commission

4.2 Commission Calculation Example

For a property sale of 2,000,000 AED:

  • Total commission: 40,000 AED (2% of sale price)
  • Agent's share: 20,000 AED (50%)
  • Referrer commission (Buyer lead at 25%): 5,000 AED
  • Referrer commission (Seller lead at 20%): 4,000 AED

4.3 Payment Terms

  • Commissions are paid within 45-60 days after the transaction is completed and the agent receives payment
  • Payment methods: Bank transfer (UAE or international)
  • Minimum payout threshold: 500 AED
  • The Referrer is responsible for any applicable taxes

4.4 Commission Eligibility

A commission is only due when:

  • The lead was submitted through the Platform
  • The lead was not previously registered by another Referrer
  • The transaction is completed within 12 months of lead submission
  • The agent receives their commission from the transaction

Each lead must include:

IMPORTANT: You must obtain the client's explicit consent before submitting their information. By submitting a lead, you confirm that:
